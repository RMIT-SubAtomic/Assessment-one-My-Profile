Interest in IT

What is your interest in I.T.?

My main interest in IT is with networks and security, I am fascinated with how the technology works from the hardware that connects everything to the software protocols that help establish how each computer talks to each other.

At the age of 12, I built my first computer, it was an AMD K5 100mhz, it took me about 6 months buying the parts bit by bit, and from that moment on my interest in computers and IT started.

Was there a particular event or person that sparked your interest?

I can remember getting my first computer book which was “Build your own PC, For Dummies” and it was like a bible for me, anytime I need help figuring out what to buy next for my computer I turned to that book if something was not working that is where I turned aswell.
